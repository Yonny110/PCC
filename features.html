Why Choose PCC?

Comprehensive features that set us apart in Catholic education

Quality Education

Academic excellence rooted in Catholic values and tradition. Our curriculum combines rigorous academics with moral and spiritual development to prepare students for success in all aspects of life.

  • Comprehensive K-12 curriculum
  • Specialized tracks in Senior High School
  • Industry-relevant college programs
  • Dedicated and qualified faculty
  • Modern teaching methodologies

Faith Formation

Nurturing spiritual growth and moral development through regular religious activities, retreats, and community service programs that strengthen Catholic identity.

  • Daily prayer and weekly Mass
  • Religious education integrated in curriculum
  • Retreats and recollections
  • Community outreach programs
  • Sacramental preparation

Modern Facilities

State-of-the-art learning environments and resources including smart classrooms, laboratories, and specialized learning spaces designed for 21st century education.

  • Fully-equipped science laboratories
  • Computer labs with latest technology
  • Comprehensive library resources
  • Sports and recreational facilities
  • Specialized learning spaces

Community Engagement

Developing socially responsible and compassionate individuals through outreach programs, community partnerships, and service-learning opportunities.

  • Community outreach programs
  • Service-learning opportunities
  • Partnerships with local organizations
  • Environmental advocacy
  • Social awareness campaigns

Qualified Faculty

Our dedicated teaching staff consists of experienced educators who are committed to student success and continuous professional development.

  • Highly qualified and experienced teachers
  • Continuous professional development
  • Student-centered teaching approach
  • Mentorship and guidance programs
  • Research and publication opportunities

Holistic Development

We focus on developing the whole person - intellectually, spiritually, emotionally, socially, and physically - through various programs and activities.

  • Academic excellence programs
  • Sports and athletics
  • Arts and culture activities
  • Leadership training
  • Character formation
