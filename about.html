About

Quality Catholic Education Since 1913

Our History

Founded in 1913, Pasig Catholic College has been a pillar of quality Catholic education in Pasig City for over a century. Established as part of the Immaculate Conception Parish, PCC has grown from humble beginnings to become a comprehensive educational institution serving students from pre-school to graduate studies.

Throughout its history, PCC has remained committed to its mission of providing education that integrates faith, learning, and living. The college has adapted to changing times while maintaining its core Catholic identity and values.

Our Identity

As a Catholic educational institution, PCC is committed to the formation of individuals who are not only academically competent but also morally upright and socially responsible. Our educational philosophy is rooted in the teachings of the Catholic Church and the rich tradition of Catholic education.

We believe that education should develop the whole person - mind, body, and spirit - and prepare students to make meaningful contributions to society while living out their faith in their daily lives.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

As we advance towards our vision, we seek to live out and maintain the following principles:

  1. We are role models in the practice of our Christian faith
  2. We discern and develop what is distinctly Catholic in our educational system
  3. We promote the value of excellence at all levels of the institution's operation
  4. As a community, we adhere to social transformation through immersion and advocacy, community outreach and parish involvement
  5. We empower our workforce by professionalizing all operations and setting up of more effective systems of coordination, communication and networking among units of the institution

Our Vision

PASIG CATHOLIC COLLEGE, as part of the Immaculate Conception Parish, envisions itself as an evangelized and evangelizing community whose commitment to quality Catholic education promotes a culture of excellence and the task of social transformation.

We strive to form individuals who are competent, compassionate, and committed to serving God and society, contributing to the building of a just and humane world.

Our Core Values

The foundation of everything we do at Pasig Catholic College

Christian Discipleship

Living our faith through piety, knowledge of Catholic teachings, faithfulness, and gratitude in all aspects of life.

Respect for Human Dignity

Upholding integrity, goodness, wellness, and cleanliness in our interactions with others.

Preferential Option for the Poor

Demonstrating empathy, justice, and courage in serving those most in need.

Excellence

Pursuing competence, resourcefulness, and discipline in all our endeavors.

Responsible Stewardship

Exercising charity, dependability, prudence, and love for the environment.

Commitment to the Local Church

Fostering communion, parish involvement, and nationalism in our community.
