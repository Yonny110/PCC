Library

Our library is well-stocked with a wide range of books, journals, and digital resources to support learning and research for all students and faculty. It offers a quiet and comfortable environment for study, group work, and academic exploration.

  • Extensive collection of academic books and references
  • Access to online journals and e-books
  • Study areas and discussion rooms
  • Computer terminals for research
  • Friendly and helpful library staff
Visit the library to discover more resources!

Crown Jewel

The Crown Jewel is a multipurpose area at Pasig Catholic College, designed for community gatherings, school events, and special occasions. It highlights the school's Catholic identity and serves as a venue for spiritual, cultural, and social engagement.

  • Spacious hall for assemblies and celebrations
  • Equipped with modern audio-visual facilities
  • Hosts religious, academic, and social events
  • Comfortable seating and flexible arrangements
  • Accessible to students, faculty, and guests
Experience the heart of PCC's community life at the Crown Jewel!

Computer Laboratories

Our state-of-the-art computer laboratories provide students with hands-on IT education, programming experience, and digital project development. Equipped with modern hardware and software to support collaborative learning and technological innovation.

  • Modern computer systems with latest software
  • High-speed internet connectivity
  • Programming and development environments
  • Collaborative workstations for group projects
  • Supervised by experienced IT instructors
Develop your digital skills in our advanced computer labs!

St. Joseph Gymnasium

The St. Joseph Gymnasium is a versatile sports facility that hosts various athletic events, school gatherings, and physical education activities. With its spacious design and modern amenities, it serves as a hub for physical development and community events.

  • Full-size basketball court with professional flooring
  • Volleyball and badminton court markings
  • Bleacher seating for spectators
  • Sound system for events and announcements
  • Equipment storage and locker rooms
Stay active and healthy in our well-equipped gymnasium!

Campus Chapel

The Campus Chapel provides a sacred space for worship, prayer, and spiritual reflection for the PCC community. It serves as the heart of our Catholic identity, offering daily Mass, prayer services, and moments of quiet contemplation.

  • Daily Mass and liturgical celebrations
  • Quiet space for personal prayer and reflection
  • Religious icons and artwork
  • Confessionals and spiritual guidance
  • Seasonal decorations and liturgical elements
Nourish your spiritual life in our peaceful chapel!

Aula Minor

The Aula Minor is a versatile event space designed for school performances, presentations, seminars, and large gatherings. With its professional stage setup and comfortable seating, it provides an ideal venue for academic and cultural events.

  • Professional stage with lighting system
  • Audio-visual equipment for presentations
  • Comfortable seating for large audiences
  • Backstage area for performers
  • Flexible arrangement for various events
Experience memorable events in our Aula Minor!
