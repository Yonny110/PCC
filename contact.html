Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common inquiries

What are the admission requirements?
Admission requirements vary by program level. Generally, you'll need completed application forms, academic records, birth certificate, and other supporting documents. Please contact the Admissions Office for specific requirements.
Do you offer scholarships?
Yes, we offer various scholarships including academic, athletic, and financial aid scholarships. Contact our Accounting Office for more information about eligibility and application process.
What are the office hours?
Our offices are open Monday to Friday from 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM, and Saturdays from 8:00 AM to 12:00 NN. We are closed on Sundays and holidays.
